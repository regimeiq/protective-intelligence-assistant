## Recommended Review Queue Decision

Queue status: **Review**

Recommended analyst actions:

1. Confirm the latest travel advisory and UK threat-level page within 72 hours of travel.
2. Collect itinerary-specific details: venues, transport modes, arrival/departure windows, public appearances, and hotel exposure.
3. For public events, request organizer safety plan, stewarding model, emergency procedures, and local authority coordination points.
4. Reduce unnecessary public-area dwell time at transport hubs and crowded venues.
5. Document assumptions and confidence in the casepack before any escalation decision.
